Programmes:

Good to great:

This is a programme designed to assist top management and leaders transform their organizations from good organisations to great organisations that are equipped to compete in the global market. The programme is research based and principle-centered. It is an excellent input into organisations that have developed or are in the process of developing their strategic plans.

Leadership and Management Development Programme:

This is a 5-day experiential learning programme for middle and top-level management. It covers topics such as:

  • Corporate Governance.
  • Leadership and Management.
  • Communication.
  • Feedback.
  • Conflict transformation and management.
  • Group decision making & problem solving.
  • Team-building and group dynamics.
  • Strategic Planning.

Other Programmes:

  • Leading Change.
  • Personal effectiveness (habits of highly effective people).
  • Negotiation skills.
  • Time and life management.
  • Mediation skills.
  • Communication, including transactional analysis (TA).
  • Coaching.
  • Training trainers.
  • Performance management.
  • Project management leadership.
  • Emotional intelligence.
  • Organisational culture.
  • Values.
  • Assertiveness.
  • Effective oral presentations.
