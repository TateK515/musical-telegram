About Professor Maphosa:

Professor Norman Maphosa is a highly qualified organizational development (OD) consultant with over 35 years experience in leadership and management in the private and public sectors. His leadership experience has been in spearheading the establishment of Solusi University as Vice Chancellor, and seeing its' development into a respected institution of higher learning in Zimbabwe and the (SADC) region. He also played a role in the establishment of the Zimbabwe institute of Public Administration and management (ZIPAM).

Professor Maphosa has also served in the following capacities:

  • Director General of ZIPAM.
  • Member of the Zimbabwe Council of Higher Education (ZIMCHE).
  • Chairman of the Zimbabwe School Examination Council (ZIMSEC).
